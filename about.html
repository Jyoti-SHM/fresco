At Fresco Hotel, located in the beautiful surroundings of Haridwar, Uttarakhand, we recognize that many of our clients visit for spiritual and religious reasons. Set against the backdrop of the Himalayas and the sacred Ganges River, our hotel offers a peaceful retreat. Whether you're here to attend the Ganga Aarti, embark on a pilgrimage, or simply seek tranquility, our comfortable rooms, authentic cuisine, and warm hospitality ensure a memorable stay. Experience the essence of spirituality and relaxation at Fresco Hotel, where every visit is a journey of peace and rejuvenation.
